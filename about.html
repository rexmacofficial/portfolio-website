REX MACAPINLAC · BOSTON, MA

Music ignited my love for visual design. As a kid, good grades meant trips to FYE or Strawberries where my mom would treat me to a CD of my choosing. I remember unfolding the booklets and studying the colors, fonts, and photos inside. Linkin Park's “Hybrid Theory.” Pharrell's “In My Mind.” Kanye West's “Graduation.” The look always matched how the album sounded; enhanced it even. Years later, I'm grateful I still get to chase this feeling -- and capture it, as a designer myself, by helping organizations define their visual identity and tell effective stories.

Since 2017, I've served as a UX/UI Designer, Website and Social Media Manager, and Video Editor.

Previously, I redesigned and managed the website for a Bank in Worcester, MA and directed multiple advertising campaigns. For a children's hospital in Boston, MA, I revamped signage and video displays across its entire campus and created videos on patient stories for its social media. Currently, I serve as a Website and Social Media Specialist for the Massachusetts Commission for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing.

In 2019, I was the youngest honoree recognized among Worcester Business Journal's 40 Under Forty, which highlighted Central Massachusetts' 40 most impactful business leaders under the age of 40.

I am still ever passionate about nailing the look and feel. Please click below to view my resume in greater detail. Thanks for visiting!
